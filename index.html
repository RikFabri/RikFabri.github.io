EasyTextures

Description

Easy textures is a small (2 files) plugin that allows users to drag and drop an image into Maya to use it as a texture. Much like the behaviour in 3dsMax. Next to automatically creating and applying the shaders, the plugin also keeps track of the existing ones to prevent duplicates.

NOX

Description

Nox is a 2,5D atmospheric puzzle platform game set in an 80's arcade. Made for the DAE course "game projects".


Team

We were a team of 2 programmers and 4 artists (Both me and the other programmer had no prior unreal C++ experience). Everyone put in around 80-100 hours. I myself did most of the gameplay programming, including:


  • Pushing/Pulling
  • Spiderwebs that lock objects
  • Climbing
  • Ziplining
  • Rope splinemeshes
  • General purpose levers, easily extensible in blueprints for our level designers
  • Bossfight (generating leg movement through IK, spider-pushable and spider-player interactions)
